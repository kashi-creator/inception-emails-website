About Inception Emails

AI-powered cold email outreach built for B2B service businesses that are tired of overpaying for paid ads.

What we do

Inception Emails is a done-for-you cold email outreach service. We use AI to research prospects across firmographic, technographic, and behavioral dimensions, write each email individually (no templates, no mail merge), qualify replies, and book interested decision-makers directly onto your calendar.

Our clients are service businesses across six verticals: managed service providers (MSPs), dental practices, functional medicine clinics, life insurance agents, mortgage brokers, and property maintenance companies. Each vertical gets industry-tailored messaging and ICP targeting.

Why it works

Most cold email fails because it is templated. Generic openers, copy-pasted value props, identical CTAs. Prospects can spot a template in two seconds and delete it.

Inception Emails uses AI to write each email individually based on the specific prospect's role, company, recent activity, and likely pain points. The result is a message that reads like a real human researched the prospect and wrote them specifically — because that is functionally what happened, just done at scale.

How we are different from paid ads

  • Cost. $15–40 per qualified lead vs $150–300 on Google Ads, $80–200 on Meta.
  • Intent depth. A reply to a personalized email signals deeper intent than a paid-ad click.
  • Compounding. AI optimization compounds on real reply data over time. Paid ads do not.
  • Predictability. Once a campaign is dialed in, monthly qualified-lead volume is predictable. Paid ads are subject to platform cost spikes and algorithm changes.
